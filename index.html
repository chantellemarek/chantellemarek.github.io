I'm a Computer Science student at Lakeland University

My name is Chantelle Marek. Currently, I am attending Lakeland University to earn my Bachelors Degree in Computer Science and Cybersecurity. My prior education includes earning my Associates Degree from Chippewa Valley Technical College. While at CVTC, I majored in I.T. - Software Development.


My passion for web development started while at CVTC. I enjoyed learning the many technologies used to create a website. Besides working on websites, I enjoy working on Android apps. Both allow me to be creative while still writing code.
